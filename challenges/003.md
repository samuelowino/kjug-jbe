
# Challenge 3: Shopping Cart Calculator

## Business Requirement
Provide an e-commerce checkout calculator that applies customer loyalty discounts and product category taxes correctly.

## Endpoint

| Method | Endpoint | Business Purpose |
|--------|----------|------------------|
| POST | `/cart/calculate` | Calculate final price after discounts and taxes |

## Business Rules

### Customer Loyalty Discounts
- Standard customers: No discount
- Silver members: 5% off entire purchase
- Gold members: 10% off entire purchase

### Product Category Taxes
- Books: 0% tax (educational materials are tax-exempt)
- Food: 5% tax (reduced rate for essentials)
- Electronics: 15% tax (standard rate for luxury goods)
- Other: 10% tax (default rate for unclassified items)

## Business Requirements

### Requirement 1: Validate shopping cart contents
The system must reject carts that:
- Contain no items
- Have items with zero or negative quantity
- Have items with zero or negative price
- Have items without a name

### Requirement 2: Validate customer tier
The system must only recognize Standard, Silver, or Gold customer tiers. Unknown tiers must be rejected.

### Requirement 3: Validate product category
The system must only recognize Books, Food, Electronics, or Other categories. Unknown categories must be rejected.

### Requirement 4: Calculate subtotal correctly
The system must compute subtotal as sum of (price × quantity) for all items.

### Requirement 5: Apply discount based on loyalty tier
The system must subtract the appropriate discount percentage based on the customer's membership level.

### Requirement 6: Apply tax per product category
The system must calculate tax for each item based on its category, then sum all taxes.

### Requirement 7: Calculate final total
The system must compute: (Subtotal - Discount) + Tax = Final Total

### Requirement 8: Present clear breakdown
The system must return subtotal, discount amount, amount after discount, tax amount, and final total.

### Requirement 9: Handle currency rounding
The system must round all monetary values to 2 decimal places for proper currency display.

## Acceptance Tests

```bash
# Test 1: Gold member buys tax-exempt books
POST /cart/calculate
Body: {"items":[{"name":"Programming Guide","price":25,"quantity":2,"category":"BOOKS"}],"userTier":"GOLD"}
Expected: Subtotal $50.00, 10% discount ($5.00), $0 tax, final $45.00

# Test 2: Silver member buys electronics
POST /cart/calculate
Body: {"items":[{"name":"Laptop","price":500,"quantity":1,"category":"ELECTRONICS"}],"userTier":"SILVER"}
Expected: Subtotal $500.00, 5% discount ($25.00), 15% tax ($75.00), final $550.00

# Test 3: Customer tries to check out empty cart
POST /cart/calculate
Body: {"items":[],"userTier":"STANDARD"}
Expected: System rejects with error about empty cart

# Test 4: Customer tries to buy zero quantity
POST /cart/calculate
Body: {"items":[{"name":"Phone","price":500,"quantity":0,"category":"ELECTRONICS"}],"userTier":"STANDARD"}
Expected: System rejects because quantity must be positive

# Test 5: System receives unknown customer tier
POST /cart/calculate

POST /cart/calculate
